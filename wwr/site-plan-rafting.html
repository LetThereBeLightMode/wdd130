Overview

Purpose

Plan Site Look, feel, and layout. It includes audience targeting, branding, style guide, navigation, and wireframes, tailored for easy trip planning and booking. The aim is to create an engaging online platform that sparks excitement for potential customers.

Audience

Target Audiece will be professors, friends, and classmates
Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)
They are people of all ages, but most importantly the type of adult that would actually afford this expense. They might like a little danger, or they might also like the idea of a very safe but fun and more relaxed adventure.
What do they want?
They want a good adventurous time, and an easy way to schedule and pay for one.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Generally they might expect to find A: Pricing and Availability and B: What a rafting trip would look and feel like.
